How DeepInsight X Works

A transparent look at the architecture, data, and logic behind our medical AI system.

The Architecture

DeepInsight X is powered by ConvNeXt Large, a modern convolutional neural network that bridges the gap between standard CNNs and Vision Transformers.

We selected ConvNeXt over ResNet-101 and EfficientNet-B4 because it offers superior accuracy on medical imaging tasks while maintaining reasonable inference speeds.

Multi-Label Classification Predicts multiple conditions simultaneously.

The Dataset

Our model was trained on the NIH ChestX-ray14 dataset, one of the largest and most comprehensive collections of chest X-rays available.

This massive scale ensures the model has seen a wide variety of patient anatomies, disease presentations, and image qualities.
